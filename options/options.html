Welcome to Gamma

Our Mission

Gamma helps you maintain mindful eating habits by using AI to detect when you're eating and checking in on your well-being.

Key Features

  • Meal Detection: Optional camera-based meal detection
  • Wellness Check-ins: Friendly prompts during meals
  • AI Assistant: Powered by advanced language models
